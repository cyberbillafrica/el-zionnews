Rivers State March 19, 2025
Akpabio visited Wike a few hours before the State of Emergency was imposed on Rivers State by Tinubu.

.It has recently been reported that the Senate President, Senator Godswill Akpabio, paid a visit to the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) Minister, Nyesom Wike, just a few hours prior to President Tinubu's imposition of a State of Emergency on Rivers State. This meeting between two prominent political figures, along with the timing of the presidential action, has sparked significant interest and speculation within political circles and among the general public.

The context surrounding this series of events raises questions and concerns about the potential influence and relationships between key political players in the country. Given the sensitive nature of political power dynamics and decision-making processes, the timing of Senator Akpabio's meeting with Minister Wike adds another layer of intrigue to the unfolding situation in Rivers State.

As the public awaits further developments and explanations from all parties involved, it is essential to closely monitor how these interactions may impact not only the political landscape in Rivers State but also the broader implications for governance and democracy in Nigeria.
