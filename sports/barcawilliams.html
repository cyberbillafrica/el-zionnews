BARCELONA ‘REACH PERSONAL AGREEMENT’ WITH NICO WILLIAMS ON SIX-YEAR DEAL

EL-ZION NEWS   

Barcelona have reportedly reached an agreement on personal terms with Nico Williams for a six-year contract with the Catalan club.

Speculation surrounding the potential move has intensified, with fresh updates suggesting the deal is progressing.

Reports indicate that the personal terms include a salary in the region of €7-8 million per season—slightly below figures previously mentioned in connection with a “pre-agreement.”

Barcelona are now focused on negotiating with Athletic Club, aware that Williams’ release clause is approximately €62 million.

Club president Joan Laporta recently hinted at the transfer in a statement made during a club meeting.

“We’re on the verge of making an imminent signing. We’ll surely be able to sign another player who excites us a lot,” he said—comments widely believed to reference the Athletic Club forward.
