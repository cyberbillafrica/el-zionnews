Five Sundays with Jesus Christ, theme , Prayer is the key. Prophetess Mrs Onyinyechi Ezekpo .

Indeed, the significance of prayers cannot be overstated. As stated in the Bible, the prayers of a righteous person are powerful and effective. Prophetess Mrs. Onyinyechi Ezekpo's upcoming program, titled "Prayer is the Key," is poised to be a transformative and spiritually uplifting experience.

The event, spearheaded by Prophetess Ezekpo, promises to delve into the profound impact of prayer in one's life and the divine power it holds. Participants can anticipate engaging sessions, fervent supplications, and teachings on deepening their prayer lives. The program is designed to equip attendees with the tools, understanding, and fervor needed to unlock the blessings and breakthroughs that come through fervent prayer.

Prophetess Mrs. Onyinyechi Ezekpo, known for her anointed ministry and dedication to spiritual growth, brings a wealth of experience and insight to this program. Those who attend can expect to be edified, encouraged, and empowered in their prayer journey. "Prayer is the Key" is not just an event; it is an opportunity for individuals to draw closer to the divine and experience the transformative power of prayer in their lives.

The upcoming event titled "Five Sundays with Jeans Christ: Prayer is the Key" promises to be a spiritually enriching experience that you definitely do not want to miss. This program features renowned Gospel artists such as Minister Engr Destiny Onovo, Evang Chinonso, and Evang Chidubem who will lead the congregation in a powerful session of praise and worship.

Scheduled to take place from Sunday, March 2nd, to Sunday, March 30th, 2025, at the Divine Favour Prophetic and Healing Power Ministry Auditorium located at No. 58 Kenyetha Street, Uwani, Enugu, Enugu State, Nigeria, this event will run from 4:00 pm to 6:30 pm each Sunday.

For inquiries and seat reservations, please contact the organizers at +2348063916172. This is an opportunity for believers to come together in fellowship and worship, seeking spiritual renewal and strength. Mark your calendars and make plans to attend this special event that promises to be uplifting and inspiring.
