Minister Anthony NC releases a hit track.Titled the GROANING SPIRIT. Details……..

It is truly remarkable to witness the impact that Mr. Anthony Chima Nwazue, also known as Anthony NC, has had on the youth through his Holy ghost chant music. His influence as a gospel artist has been nothing short of inspirational, uplifting the souls of many with his powerful songs. In a recent interview conducted by El-zion Global Network Tv/News, Mr. Anthony NC shared his thoughts and experiences as a gospel artist, shedding light on his journey and the message he hopes to convey through his music.During the interview, Mr. Anthony NC delved into the creative process behind his soul-stirring songs, revealing the spiritual inspiration that drives his music. His dedication to spreading positivity and faith through his artistry was evident, as he shared anecdotes of the impact his music has had on listeners. His commitment to uplifting and inspiring the youth through his Holy ghost chant music is truly commendable, and his sincerity and passion shine through in every word he speaks.Overall, Mr. Anthony NC's unwavering dedication to his craft and his ability to touch the hearts of many with his music make him a true beacon of hope and inspiration in the gospel music industry.

Could you please let us know your name, Ii mean your real name.

My name is Nwazuo Anthony Chima.

What is your stage name?

Anthony Nc is my stage name.

What is your Birthday and month?

10th of February.

What is your state of origin in Nigeria?

Abia state is my state of origin.

What actually got you inspired into singing or becoming a Music artist?

when I was still tender, growing up in my home town Umuahia in Abia State, l had love for music, then I have not given my life to Jesus, so i use to love secular hip pop music so much, but the funny thing I discovered with me then is that whenever I opened my mouth to sing, I see myself singing songs that have not been recorded by anyone, I will quickly right it down and record it with my small radio that has recording ability then. Sometimes when I wake up early in the morning or in the bathroom taking my bath I will be hearing new songs playing in my mind I will quickly write them down and record them with my radio, that was how I was able to composed about sixty something tracks then, but all of them were secular songs, me and 3 of my friends that were in to music had a crew by name "fortune boys" that was around 2008 and my favorite artist then was 2face idibia before I encountered Jesus Christ. I remembered then encountering one pastor that has love for the youth then by name pastor Elijah onyador"bishop" , around 2009, and when he discovered I had gift in music, because he is into music too, he encouraged me to start composing Christian songs which I started, so he started featuring me in his youth programs as a music minister. Immediately I formed a crew by the name eminent brother's and we started attending and ministering in different churches at night vigils then in Umuahia Abia state, and people started liking us based on the uniqueness of our songs because they were new songs composed by me. That was how I was moving before I encountered Jesus through one book i read titled "prepare to meet your God" around 2011 that was how I started going through bible in other to confirm whether the things written in that book were true or not because am not use to Bible before then, and that was how I fell in love with the word of God, from that moment I started having many encounter in my dreams, and one of the days, the Spirit of God lead me to the father of the pastor that encouraged me to start composing Christian songs, pastor Elijah onyador, around 2012. The father happened to be the general overseer of their church then and he led me to Christ together with some of my friends that I was able to convince through my encounters and dreams. From that very day I stopped everything about music, destroyed my music books both secular and Christian songs that I composed then and started loving and devoting my time to Bible studies, fasting, prayer and evangelism. Through my devoted Bible studies I came to discover some truth in the word of God that lead me out from Catholic church to a branch of one Pentecostal church in umuahia Abia state by name Christian charismatic ministry and after sometime the Spirit of God lead me out from my hometown Umuahia to Enugu state and connected me to my father in the lord pastor orji victor chukwuma the Enugu regional pastor of Christian charismatic ministry for my proper training process that was around 2013 and I started serving under him. After the end of my discipleship class he got me Baptised by immersion and Holy Ghost and I started working as evangelist under him One of the days i went to a community called ugwuaji in Enugu state for evangelism, when I was on my way back, I had this strong burden in my heart, lord what can I do to be able to gather this community together in other to hear your word and the Spirit of the Lord spoke to me that He has given me a gift that can help me achieve that purpose. immediately I remembered my music gift, and from that day inspirations started flowing again and I started composing songs by the aspiration of the Holy Ghost that was around 2016.

That was a rare and deep encounter with God, honestly

Yes of course I really appreciate God for such Grace

What do we call this type of song you have chosen?

This type of song is called Western song /chants

As a growing Child, who's your favorite actor and singer?

My favorite actor as of the time I encountered God is daddy mike bamiloye of mount zion film and my favorite singer as of then was Chioma Jesus, Samsong, Sinech and Frank Edward.

What actually inspired you into becoming a singer?

What actually inspired me into becoming a singer was when I discovered I had singing as a gift from the Lord, for the purpose of helping me express my love, Thanksgiving, praise and worship unto my God in my closet, and also a tool to call many together unto the Lord.

Who is the artist that got you inspired most?

The ministers that always gets me inspired whenever I listens to their songs are minister thiophilus Sunday, minister Guc and minister Ebuka songs.

From where do you often draw your inspiration ?

I often draw my inspirations from the Holy Spirit through God's word. My prayer altar. from anointed messages like Apostle Arome osayi , Apostle Joshua selman, Apostle Michael oropko, minister thiophilus Sunday, Apostle Edu udechukwu, mini Ebuka song and many other Remnant Christian network ministers.

Was your parents or Guardian fully in support of your crave towards music when you started displaying your love towards music?

Yes my parents have been one of the people that has been encouraging me, although it wasn't like this at the early stage because they were not seeing music as something one should depends on, while God told me that he will make a way for me through His gift in me, but only when they started seeing the hand of the lord upon my life, that was when they started supporting me

How far do you intend to go as a music artist?

Actually, by the special grace of God and the help of the Holy Ghost I will get to the peak of my calling in music and also becoming a ladder to the young ones.

What year did you start composing, and singing in the public.?

I started composing secular songs first when I have not encountered Jesus from 2005, 2006. Then after I encountered Jesus from 2012 I left music entirely until around 2016 when I started composing songs again by the inspiration of the Holy Ghost, after hearing from God

We heard that you recently dropped a track. What's the title, can you tell us a bit about the song and the inspiration behind the song?

Yes, I dropped two songs by the help of God, one is a song titled JESUS I LOVE YOU and the second one is a prayer chant titled THE GROANING SPIRIT. The first song Jesus I love you helped me to express my love toward my lover, it came when I was meditating over the love, goodness and mercy of the Lord towards my life, His saving power that came through for me when I was totally lost in sin, when I was still staggering in weakness, He never let me go, even when I saw myself in the the valley of shadow of death thinking that my own chapter is closed, suddenly His love came through for me and lifted me. So is a sound that helps me declare my love and confidence in Him and Him alone. The second one The groaning spirit was a prayer chant I received when we were about starting an interdenominational and nondenominational gathering for young people in my home town Umuahia, so we have already fixed our first night vigil, so I was in my room praying, meditating and asking God how we are to go on the very day of that night vigil, and suddenly the song came assuring me that the Holy Ghost will be with us to intercede on our behalf, and we raised it during the night vigil the atmosphere opened immediately and we were all energized, and after we recorded it in the studio I had a revelation when we were in a prayer conference filled with people, and the Groaning Spirit chant was been played in the hall and I saw Apostle Joshua Selman praying and laying hands on us and after some months my covenant brother minister Ikechukwu kelechi had a similar revelation two.

So far, which of your songs do you consider as the hit track?

The Groaning Spirit chant

Are you currently under any music label?

No

How far do you want to go as a music artist?

by the special grace of God I will not go more than or below what God has ordained for me in my music ministry.

Who is your best Music artist in Nigeria?

My favorite music artists in Nigeria are minister thiophilus, Guc and Ebuka song

.

On a global scene, who is your favorite artist? Michael Smith of hill song

have you been invited for a show before or have you been able to organize one before?

When I was in Enugu we often used to organize youth worship prayer word retreats from time to time in various churches I was privileged to serve, in the Old path revival commission abapka branch. Shelter in the storm assembly old unth branch Enugu north. RCN Enugu during the inauguration preparation prayer stretch caption "The rise of true witness" and many others, and we are currently running an interdenominational and nondenominational gathering here in umuahia abia state 2nd and last Sunday of every month by 4pm with a retreat program that comes 3 times every year by the help us the Holy Ghost.

It's often believed that song artists are those that have a blocked head, I mean those that couldn't fit in academically. What is your view about that?

I don't believe that to be true, because we have music as one of the art courses in many high institutions in the world. Secondly we have many graduate that are in music industry

Are you open for an invitation for a show, even outside the country?

Yes definitely am open for an invitations here in Nigeria and beyond, as long as the Spirit of God accept to go with us, for our target is unto the outermost part of the earth by God's grace

In the next 2 years where are you seeing yourself and your song career?

I am seeing myself and my music career at the peak of what God has for me in 2 years from now as long as Christ tarries
